Who We Are

At Tutum Tech Security Services, we believe that security isn't just a service - it's a strategic necessity. Founded in 2019 by Mr. Geoffrey Falinya, we are a Malawi-registered security company headquartered in Lilongwe, behind Walkers Total Filling Station.

We provide Security Science Service (SSS) - an advanced and holistic approach that ensures total protection of life, assets and property. Unlike traditional security models, SSS integrates intelligence, proactivity and ethics to eliminate the risk of crime and moral compromise.

With a growing portfolio of satisfied clients and a track record of excellence, we are committed to setting the standard for security management across Malawi and beyond.
