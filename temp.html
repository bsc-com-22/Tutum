Who We Are

At Tutum Tech Security Services, protection is our business. We are a trusted security partner for businesses, institutions, and individuals across Malawi.

With a commitment to safety, professionalism, and innovation, we deliver tailored security solutions that include corporate security management, personal protection, K9 units, and advanced security technology systems.

Our experienced team works around the clock to ensure your peace of mind—because your safety is not just our priority, it's our promise.
